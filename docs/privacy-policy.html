531 Strength — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

531 Strength is a personal fitness app. Your privacy is important — this app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

All workout data, settings, and exercise history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to external servers.

HealthKit

This app integrates with Apple HealthKit to:

  • Read heart rate data during workouts
  • Save completed workouts to Apple Health
  • Save estimated calorie data to Apple Health

HealthKit data is only accessed with your explicit permission and is never shared with third parties. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings > Health > 531 Strength.

Backup & Restore

The app offers a manual backup feature that exports your data as a JSON file. This file is saved to a location you choose (e.g., iCloud Drive, Files). The app does not automatically upload data anywhere.

Analytics & Tracking

This app does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.
